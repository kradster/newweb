PROBLEMS WITH TRADING


COMPLEXITY

Millions of traders have the potential to earn on marginal trading with high leverage. Trading in this form allows for high yields, but with corresponding high risks involved. Different assets used for these transactions include currencies, cryptocurrencies, stock, index and commodity contracts for difference (CFDs) and other instruments.

UNCERTAINTY

With the seemingly innumerable altcoins ICOs, exchanges and next generation blockchain forks in byzantine-like growth, how does one ensure fair transactions without breaking the bank?

VULNERABILITY

A significant number of traders are not protected from fraud by intermediaries (such as brokers, dealers and even exchanges themselves).

OBSOLESCENCE

In summary, traditional regulation of the market is ineffective, outdated, and cannot be relied upon to protect investments of retail clients.
Nevertheless, blockchain technology can solve these problems. That is why we created D'Crypt™.






SOLUTION - D'CRYPT (TM) PLATFORM

SIMPLIFIED

SAFE AND SECURE

We ensure security for individual buyers’ and sellers’ funds by placing them on D'Crypt™ smart contracts based on the ERC-20 blockchain.

The contract is created and structured to ensure that funds transfer proceeds only by the mutual consent of the buyer and the seller directly removing the need of a broker. If a conflict occurs, D'Crypt™ can mediate in the transaction.
